Algorithm & Methodology

Melting Temperature (Wallace Rule)

Tm = 2 × (A + T) + 4 × (G + C)

Simplified formula for primers under 25 bp. Assumes 50mM Na⁺ concentration.

Penalty Score Formula

penalty = (|fwd_tm - 60| × 0.05) + (|rev_tm - 60| × 0.05)
+ (|fwd_gc - 50| × 0.03) + (|rev_gc - 50| × 0.03)
+ ((10 - quality) × 0.1)

Lower penalty indicates better primer pair. Targets: Tm = 60°C, GC = 50%.

Design Constraints

Parameter Min Max Optimal
Primer Length 18 bp 25 bp 20 bp
GC Content 40% 65% 50%
Melting Temp 55°C 65°C 60°C
Product Size 150 bp 400 bp 200-300 bp

Quality Scoring Features

  • Homopolymer penalty: -4.0 for 5+ repeats
  • G/C clamp bonus: +0.5 at 3' end
  • Poly-N penalty: -3.0 for ambiguous bases
  • Repeat penalty: -1.5 for dinucleotide repeats
